We accept donations in a variety of formats. We accept Ethereum, Bitcoin, Litecoin, Paypal, Credit and Debit (via Paypal).

Ethereum donations can be sent directly to a specific book from our browse page. If you would like a Bitcoin or Litecoin donation to head to specific works, please include the ID(s) in a message attached to your transaction. If you would like USD donations to head to a specific work, please include the ID(s) in the form when making your donation. We take 30% of donations given towards specific works in order to support general operations and expansion. This 30% is automatically taken from Ethereum donations and will be manually taken from other donations.

The holocron.foundation is recognized as a nonprofit by the state of Ohio, but are not recognized as a 501(c)(3) by the IRS. We are in the process of applying to become a 501(c)(3). Should we recieve that recognition, all donations, past and future, will be considered tax deductible. We are not guaranteeing that we will be deemed a 501(c)(3) by the IRS.
